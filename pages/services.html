Professional Trademark Registration

Securing a trademark registry entry involves navigating multi-jurisdiction laws, Nice Class classifications, and proof of use files. Our attorneys assume total operational control of this process, translating governmental requirements into straightforward steps.

Filing Stages & Timeline:
  • Filing Prep: Nice class analysis, specimen verification, and owner detail compilation.
  • Examination: Government attorneys review distinctiveness and potential conflicts (takes 4-6 months).
  • Publication: 30-to-90-day public window for third-party oppositions.

99.4%

Filing Success Rate

We manage USPTO, WIPO (Madrid System), EUIPO, and national registries globally.

Trademark Maintenance & Renewal

Prevent expiration and forfeiture. We maintain permanent monitoring of registration milestones, executing renewal declarations and affidavits of use seamlessly.

Critical Milestones:
  • Years 5-6 (Section 8): Declaration of Continued Use proving the mark is active.
  • Years 9-10 (Section 8 & 9): Combined declaration and renewal request (recurs every 10 years).
  • Incontestability (Section 15): Filed after 5 years of continuous use to secure absolute ownership status.

10 Years

Validity Term per Renewal

Automated tracking ensures you never miss a filing window and risk losing your brand name.
