WIPO Base Fee Adjustments for International Madrid Applications

USPTO RULING June 05, 2026

USPTO Releases Guidelines Regarding AI-Generated Logos

New federal guidelines confirm that design files containing purely machine-generated graphics without human modification lack the distinctiveness required for statutory trademark shielding. A modification audit is mandatory to ensure logo asset distinctiveness before registry submission.
